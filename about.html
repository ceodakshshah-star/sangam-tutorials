Mamta Purswani

Mamta Purswani is the founder of Sangam Tutorials and a passionate educator dedicated to nurturing young minds through conceptual learning and curiosity. With years of teaching experience, she believes that strong fundamentals, confidence, and discipline are the pillars of academic success. Through Sangam Tutorials, she aims to create a supportive environment where students develop curiosity, clarity of concepts, and confidence in their abilities.

Our Vision

Our vision at Sangam Tutorials is to create a learning environment where every student feels encouraged to ask questions, explore ideas, and develop a deep understanding of concepts. We aim to nurture confident learners who are prepared not only for exams but also for future academic challenges.

Our Core Values

Conceptual Clarity

We focus on deep understanding of subjects rather than memorization.

Personal Attention

Every student receives individual guidance to help them improve.

Confidence Building

We help students develop confidence in their abilities and thinking.

Curiosity & Exploration

Students are encouraged to ask questions and explore new ideas.

  • Interactive teaching methods
